What is a cookie file

Cookies are text files stored by the browser you use to access https://arxall.com, on the device you access the website from. These files store information that allow us to customize and improve your navigation experience on https://arxall.com, as well as to offer you personalized information and services outside https://arxall.com.

What types of cookies we use

Mandatory
